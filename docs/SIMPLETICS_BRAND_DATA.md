## How to Use This in Ads

### Headline Formula
[Benefit] + [Simplicity/Clarity] — Always emphasize how EASY and STRAIGHTFORWARD it is

Examples:
- "Instant texture. Zero effort."
- "Texture powder that lifts. Nothing else needed."
- "Controlled texture. Transparent ingredients."

### Body Copy Tone
- Short, declarative sentences
- Avoid unnecessary adjectives
- Lead with what it DOES, not what it IS
- Include one honest limitation or honest fact (builds trust)

### CTA (Always All-Caps)
- SHOP NOW
- GET TEXTURE
- TRY THE SPRAY
- SIMPLIFY YOUR ROUTINE
